my portfolio.
Personal Art
I properly started my journey into art back in 2019 right before COVID hit. I am the type to dabble with different kinds of art styles as well as rendering methods, though you can see in most of my works that I've mostly grown a liking to a more "anime" style. Despite this though, I still continue to experiment with the kinds of art I can do and try to improve on what I've learned in the process.
Chibi Art and Stickers
I like to make cute chibi fanart and have also opened commissions for them since they're also something fun to make. I also like to make stickers and hope to be able to sell them someday in booths.
Hestia Prime Pharmaceuticals
As someone who has been with two of the companies under Hestia Prime, I've also completed different kinds of tasks for Hestia itself. I created pubmats, videos, and even made their main logo from scratch. You can see most of these works below.
Bilibenta (2023)
This is the project that was assigned to me and my fellow interns when we were going through our On-the-Job Training or Internship at SABR Solutions Inc. back in August - November 2023. For this project, I was mainly assigned to do UI/UX designs using Figma which I then helped our lead programmer implement using Vue. I and my other UI/UX designer team member also conducted a lot of research based on what the app's target audience may want the most. We created plans as well as methodically designed the app based on that research which was also done in collaboration with the initial research team.
Paraluman Skin (2023)
Paraluman Skin is another company under Hestia Prime Pharmaceuticals. During our On-the-Job Training or Internship with them, I was also given the chance to help them in their promotional materials which included stickers and promotional videos. After the internship period, they also requested that we help them created 3D models as well as designs for their upcoming makeup line.
MedRx (2021)
MedRx was one of the first projects by Cleio Multimedia Productions that was unfortunately discontinued. For this project, I created the logo and other illustrations for the project using Adobe Illustrator and created pubmats as well as the branding boards on Adobe Photoshop.
Cleio Multimedia Productions
Cleio Multimedia Productions was the foundation of SABR Solutions Inc. though it was a company more based on making multimedia outputs, including branding — Hestia Prime Pharmaceuticals was our first client. I was personally tasked with a lot of multimedia tasks which included illustration, photo editing, video editing, videography, and graphic design.
Dysglopia
Dysglopia was one of SABR Solution Inc.'s biggest projects. It was meant to be a turn-based PVP game that revolved around a post-apocalyptic world that had now been populated with "Gloops". I was the team leader for the creatives of this project and my responsibilities included graphic design, concept art, 2D illustration, 2d animation, character design, and the dissemination of tasks.
